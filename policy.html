Privacy Policy

At Bulk Mail Sender, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We do not permanently store your personal information. When you sign in with Google, we temporarily access your email address and profile information solely to authenticate your identity. We do not store, sell, or share this data.

If you upload a recipient CSV file, this data is processed in real-time to send your emails and is not stored on our servers after the process is complete.

Data Security

We use robust security measures to protect your information during transmission. All data is handled securely and is not retained after your session ends.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with the updated effective date.
