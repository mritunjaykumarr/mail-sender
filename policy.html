Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 28, 2025

At Bulk Mail Sender, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how it's used, and the security measures we take to protect it. Our practices are designed to comply with Google's API Services User Data Policy to ensure a safe and trustworthy experience.

Information We Collect

We do not permanently store your personal information. When you sign in with your Google Account, we receive and process the following data only for the duration of your session:

  • Your email address and profile information: This is used solely to authenticate your identity and to display your name and email on the application screen. We do not store or share this data.
  • Access to your Gmail account: We request permission to send emails on your behalf. This is the core functionality of our service. We use this access to send the emails you compose to the recipients you provide. We do not read, store, or process any other emails from your account.
  • Recipient Email Addresses and Email Content: When you upload a CSV file with email addresses and compose your message, this data is processed in real-time. It is used exclusively to send your bulk email and is not stored on our servers after the process is completed.

How We Use Your Information

The sole purpose for which we collect and process your data is to provide the bulk email sending service. We do not use this data for any other purpose, including:

  • Selling, trading, or renting your personal information.
  • Using your information for targeted advertising.
  • Analyzing your data for market research or other commercial purposes.

Data Security and Retention

We use robust security measures to protect your information during transmission and processing. All data is handled securely and is not retained on our servers after your session ends. We do not maintain a database of your personal information, email content, or recipient lists.

You can revoke our access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account Security Page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
